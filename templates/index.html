How to Use This Website

To use this website, simply upload an image of a pigmented lesion. The image will be classified into one of the following categories:

  • Actinic keratoses and intraepithelial carcinoma / Bowen's disease (AKIEC)
  • Basal cell carcinoma (BCC)
  • Benign keratosis-like lesions (solar lentigines / seborrheic keratoses and lichen-planus like keratoses, BKL)
  • Dermatofibroma (DF)
  • Melanoma (MEL)
  • Melanocytic nevi (NV)
  • Vascular lesions (angiomas, angiokeratomas, pyogenic granulomas and hemorrhage, VASC)

The classification process is powered by a YOLOv5 model trained on the HAM10K dataset, providing accurate and reliable results.
